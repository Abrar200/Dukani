Return and Refund Policy

1. Introduction

At Dukani Ethnic Store, we value your satisfaction and aim to provide the best possible shopping experience. This Return and Refund Policy outlines our procedures for returns, refunds, and the process for requesting them.

2. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, please ensure that:

  • The item is unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • The item is in its original packaging
  • You have the receipt or proof of purchase
  • The return is initiated within 14 days of receiving the item

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Digital products
  • Gift cards
  • Intimate or sanitary goods, once unsealed

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Dukani Ethnic Store account
  2. Navigate to the order for which you're requesting a refund
  3. Click on "Request Refund" and provide your reasoning
  4. Submit your request

Once submitted, we will send your refund request and reasoning to the business owner for review. The business owner will then make a decision regarding your refund request.

5. Refund Approval and Processing

If your refund is approved, it will be processed through the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note that it may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

6. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs for returning items are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to our error (you received an incorrect or defective item).

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will work with you to resolve the issue, which may include replacing the item or providing a full refund, including shipping costs.

8. Cancellations

For orders that have not yet shipped, you may request a cancellation. Please contact us as soon as possible with your order number and cancellation request.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Return and Refund Policy, please contact us at:

Dukani Ethnic Store
Email: dukaniethnicstore@outlook.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website.
